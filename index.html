  • Key Definitions

    - Farmstead:
    A managed ecosystem that supports* human life.
    Appropriate Technology:
    Practical, affordable tech suited to local needs and resources.
    Vital Domains:
    Eco (food, water, shelter, health, maintenance, etc.)
    - Robust & Sustainable:
    Designed to be resilient, long-lasting, and environmentally responsible for future generations.

    Independent: By independent, we mean a system that can operate and sustain itself without external inputs.
