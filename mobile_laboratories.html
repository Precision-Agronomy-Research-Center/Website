Mobile Laboratories


In partnership with our community, we are developing a fleet of mobile laboratories to support for rapid field ecology, veterinary medicine, healthcare, and community outreach. These labs are intended to reduce friction for data collection, training, and demonstration by bringing advanced lab-grade capabilities to the field. Our laboratories are more robust and cost-effective than traditional laboratories, and can be modified to suit new needs and capabilities.

Lab_01:

Mobile Agronomy Field Station

Working with open-source agronomists at GreenLathe, local livestock and poultry smallholders in Alabama, and local agricultural policy advisory commissions, we are building clinical-grade mobile laboratories for on-site field ecological testing through PARC. We have already deployed one mobile lab stocked with OpenFlexure Microscopes and poultry incubators at PARC.

Lab_02:

STEM Lab

This lab is a mobile classroom owned by the local school board and managed by Dr. Baskaran. He teaches practical agronomy curriculum, and manages lab transportation and logistics. PARC utilizes the lab to collect soil and water samples from the four K-12 schools in the district.
